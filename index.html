Game 2: Memory Dot

Do you have a good memory? Well, this is a memory based game! A blue dot appears briefly, then the screen turns dark, and players must move their character to the spot where they think the dot was at! The score is based on how close the player gets to the original location of the dot. The closer you are to the dot, the louder the volume.
